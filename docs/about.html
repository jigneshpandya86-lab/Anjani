Who We Are

Born in Vadodara,
Built for Vadodara

Anjani Premium Water is Vadodara's own packaged drinking water brand — built from the ground up to serve our city with the highest quality water at every doorstep.

We started with a simple belief: people in Vadodara deserve premium, safe, and affordable drinking water — delivered reliably, every single time.

Today, alongside our flagship Anjani 200ml brand, we also distribute Bisleri, Bailley and Clear Water — making us Vadodara's most complete water delivery solution.

Our Mission

To make pure, premium drinking water accessible to every home, hotel, office and event across Vadodara — delivered fresh, fast and with a smile.
